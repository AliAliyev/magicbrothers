About Magic Bros.

Magic Brothers Entertainment is an amateur (on the way to be professional soon) film production group. It has been established in 2012 by a group of creative guys passionate about film-making. Although we started as a film production, throughout years we further extended our activities to graphic design, illustration, painting, sound design and portrait drawing. If we had a motto, it would be something like this: "We value art more than money"

You can find more info about our team here.
