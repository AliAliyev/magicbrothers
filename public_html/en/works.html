Feature lengths

"Harry Potter and the ring of aurorship"


Our first work done back in summer of 2012. It is a fan-made no-budget Harry Potter film. The reason we chose Harry Potter is because other than film making, Harry Potter is another thing that we share in common passionately. Yeah, we are big Potterheads. Expecto Patronum!

Name: Harry Potter və Tilsimdarl1q uzuyu

Director: Ali Aliyev

Story by: Ali Aliyev

Genre: Fantasy

Language: Azerbaijani

Runtime: 41 minutes

Release date: 15 December 2012

Cast: Aydin Abbasov as Harry Potter, Emil Esedzadeh as Lord Voldermort, Cavid Rustamli as Alastor Moody, Anar Quliyev as Remus Lupin and etc.

Plot summary: A very powerful magical ring - ring of aurorship is missing. The evil and the good are fighting over to get hold of it. However, the ring has already chosen its master. Harry Potter - a normal wizard boy must suffer the consequences of being the chosen one...

The GRID

Our second work started in summer 2013. This time, we tried ourselves in science fiction. It had a lot more visual effects and required much more time and effort, so screening could take place in 2014.

Name: The GRID

Director: Ali Aliyev

Story by: Farid Huseynli, Ali Aliyev

Genre: Sci-fi, Fantasy

Language: English

Runtime: 29 minutes

Release date: 25 September 2014

Cast: Eltac Rustamli as Calvin Payton, Ali Aliyev as William Riddle, Farid Huseynli as Joe and etc.

Plot summary: Calvin Payton finds himself in the very centre of chaos when he's supposed to take exam in regular conditions instead. Now he has to face the hardest exam of his life...
